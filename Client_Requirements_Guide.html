1
Add Our Developer to Meta Business Manager
Required

Your WhatsApp number is registered on Meta's official platform. To build the interactive form (WhatsApp Flow) and connect it to your alumni database, we need to be added to your Meta Business Manager account as a developer. This is a one-time step — you simply send us an invitation.

1
Open Meta Business Manager
Go to business.facebook.com and log in with the Facebook account that owns your WhatsApp Business number.
2
Go to Business Settings
Click the Settings (⚙) icon in the left sidebar, then select "Business Settings" from the menu.
3
Open the People Section
In Business Settings, find the left menu and click on "People" under the Users section.
4
Click "Add People" or "Invite"
Click the blue "Add People" button at the top right of the page.
5
Enter Our Email Address
Type in the email address: murtaza.special623@gmail.com
Set the role to "Employee Access" — this is sufficient for our work.
6
Assign WhatsApp Account Access
On the next screen, you will be asked which assets to assign. Please select your WhatsApp Business Account and assign "Full Control" or "Manage" permission. Then click Invite.
7
Send Confirmation
Once done, please send us a quick message or email confirming the invite has been sent. We will accept it and begin the technical setup immediately.
ℹ️ Why do we need this? We need access to build the WhatsApp Form (called a "Flow") inside your account, connect it to your alumni database, and generate the API key that makes everything work automatically. We do not have access to change any of your other Facebook or Instagram pages.
⚠️ Important: Please make sure you are logged in to the Facebook account that is the admin of your WhatsApp Business number. If you are unsure which account this is, please check with whoever originally set up the WhatsApp Business API for your institution.
OR — IF YOU PREFER NOT TO ADD US AS A USER
Alternative: Share API Credentials Directly
If your team is not comfortable adding an external person to your Meta Business Manager account, you can instead share three specific credential values with us. We will use these to build and connect the system without requiring any access to your account.
1
Get Your Phone Number ID
Log in to business.facebook.com → Click WhatsApp in the left menu → Select API Setup.

You will see a field labelled "Phone Number ID" — it is a long number (e.g. 1234567890123456). Copy it and send it to us.
2
Get Your WhatsApp Business Account ID
On the same API Setup page, you will also see a field labelled "WhatsApp Business Account ID" (also called WABA ID) — another long number. Copy and send that to us as well.
3
Generate a Permanent System User Token
This is the API key that allows the system to send messages and receive responses. Follow these steps:

a) Go to Business Settings → Users → System Users
b) Click "Add" and create a new system user — name it "Alumni System", role: Employee
c) Click "Generate New Token" on that system user
d) Select your WhatsApp Business Account as the app, set expiry to Never, and tick these permissions: whatsapp_business_messaging and whatsapp_business_management
e) Click Generate Token — copy the token that appears (it is only shown once) and send it to us securely.
ℹ️ This token does not give us access to your Facebook page, Instagram, or any other platform. It only allows our system to send and receive WhatsApp messages through your registered business number. You can revoke it at any time from Business Settings → System Users.
⚠️ Security reminder: Please share the token over a secure channel — WhatsApp direct message or encrypted email. Do not paste it in a regular email or public document.

3
Share a Google Sheet for Submissions
Easiest Step

Every time an alumnus submits or updates their profile through WhatsApp, the data is instantly written to a Google Sheet. This gives your team a live, always up-to-date record of all submissions that can be downloaded as Excel at any time.

1
Create a New Google Sheet
Go to sheets.google.com and create a new blank spreadsheet. Name it something like "Amrita Alumni Verification — Submissions".
2
Click "Share" (Top Right)
Click the green "Share" button in the top right corner of the Google Sheet.
3
Add Our Email with Editor Access
In the "Add people and groups" field, type: murtaza.special623@gmail.com
Set the permission to "Editor", then click Send.
4
Copy the Sheet Link and Send It to Us
Copy the URL from your browser's address bar and send it to us. That's all — we will set up automatic data entry from WhatsApp into this sheet.
ℹ️ What will be in the sheet? Every submission will automatically appear as a new row containing: Name, Phone, Graduation Year, Campus, Department, Designation, Employer, Location, Email, LinkedIn, and the Date & Time of submission. Your team can filter, search, and download this data as Excel at any time.
